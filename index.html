My Projects

Airline Reservation System

The Airline Reservation System is a web-based platform that allows users to book flights, check availability, and manage reservations. It integrates payment systems for ticket purchases and offers real-time updates on flight schedules. Admins can manage bookings, cancellations, and view detailed reports of the flight operations.

Hostel Management System

The Hostel Management System streamlines the process of room allocation, bookings, and payments for hostel administration. It allows students to check available rooms, book accommodations, and manage their stay. Admins can track room occupancy, manage fees, and generate reports on student occupancy and payment status.

Fire Detection System

The Fire Detection System utilizes sensors to monitor temperature and smoke levels in real-time, triggering alarms in case of fire hazards. It provides automatic alerts to local authorities or fire departments, helping to mitigate risks and prevent fire-related disasters. The system is designed to ensure early detection and faster response in critical environments.
